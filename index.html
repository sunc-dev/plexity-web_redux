Transformative Care

How will we do it?

To achieve this we want to.

  • Create for ease of access.

    Provide a personal health record that is owned by you and under your control.

  • Create to be informed.

    Provide a comprehensive record that will inform you of your health information such as genetics, diagnostics, referrals and wearable data.

  • Create for peace of mind.

    Faciliate the movement of such information so that it is secure and verifiable for your peace of mind.

Challenges

Current Healthcare.

The current state of healthcare provides opportunities to explore how emerging technologies can be used to tackle these problems.

Fragmented

Personal Health information is silo’d and lacks interoperability.


Transferability

Different methods of tracking a patient’s history, makingit difficult to share at different points of care.

Reliability

No single solution that can provide authenticated patient’s data, while protecting patient privacy.

Transform

Towards Change

We are striving to building out an integrated ecosystem that will augment healthcare practices and allow doctors and patients to become more proactive with their health.

Distributed Approach

Why blockchain

Blockchain is a secure and private means of connecting multiple diverses and health system stakeholders together to support their interactions and collaboration.  


Security

A secure, lifetime health record that can be carefully shared by patients and health service providers

Verifiable

Secure authentication and identity management to ensure the right people have access to the right information to support patient care, while maintaining privacy

Reliability

Trusted transactions across the health system that enable the appropriate use and value of the patient’s health record in the care process

Fragmented

Interoperability across a diverse stakeholder and technology landscape to facilitate collaboration and flexibly ”connect” across the health system.


Digitally Recorded

Personal Health Record

Development of a “Personal” Health Record (PHR) accessible at any point of care. This record will be supported by secure linkage that authenticates healthcare providers and users.

Digitally Proactive

Predictive Care
